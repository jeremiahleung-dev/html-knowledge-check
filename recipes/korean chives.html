Best Korean Chives Recipe Out There

Korean chives side dish

Chives are a popular korean side dish that goes well with everything you eat.

The reason I learned how to make this was because markets are charging nearly 8x what the price would be to make it myself, hope this helps you save a dollar!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of chopped chives
  • 1/2 cup of soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup of sesame oil
  • 1/4 cup of minced garlic
  • 1/4 cup of minced ginger
  • 1/4 cup of chopped green onions
  • 1/4 cup of gochugaru (Korean red pepper flakes)
